Daily Calorie Needs Formula:

Katch-McArdle:
BMR = (370 + 9.8 * LBM) * Activity Level

By default, you are calculated 1g of protein per lb of LBM (Lean Body Mass) and .4g of fat per lb of TBW (Total Body Weight) if you are below 20% body fat, or .4g of fat per lb of LBM if you are above 20% body fat.

Cut and Bulk are calculated by adding or subtracting 20% from your Daily Calorie Needs.
